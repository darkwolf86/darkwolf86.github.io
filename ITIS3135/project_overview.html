TNT VR Dance Club – Project Overview

Project Overview

  1. Purpose: To create an energetic and immersive online hub for the TNT VR Dance Club — connecting members, promoting dance events, and celebrating creativity in virtual reality spaces.
  2. Intended Users: VR dance enthusiasts, club members, and anyone interested in music, movement, and digital performance.
  3. Overview of Content: The site will feature event info, media galleries, a join form, and details about the club’s activities and community goals.
