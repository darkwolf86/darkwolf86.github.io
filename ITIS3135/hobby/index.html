What are video games?

Video games are electronic games that involve interaction with a user interface to generate visual feedback on a two- or three-dimensional video display device such as a TV screen, computer monitor, or handheld device.

They can range from simple puzzles to complex virtual worlds and are enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.
