What is Photography?

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera to create images. It can be a powerful form of storytelling, documentation, or self-expression.

Whether you're using a DSLR, a phone, or a vintage film camera, photography allows you to freeze moments in time and see the world through a new lens.
