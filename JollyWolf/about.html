Jolly Wolf Barcade

Jolly Wolf is the only place in town where you can sip a perfectly balanced cocktail while blasting your favorite video game. We’re a small, family‑owned barcade that blends the charm of a retro arcade with the comfort of a craft cocktail lounge. Our mission? To give friends and strangers alike a safe, playful den where laughter echoes through the neon glow.

Whether you’re here to celebrate a birthday, unwind after work, or just test your reflexes on our classic cabinets, we’ve got something for everyone. From vintage “Pac‑Man” to modern “Fortnite” tournaments, our game selection spans decades of gaming history—each paired with a handcrafted drink that’s as bold and adventurous as you are.

We’re more than a bar or an arcade; we’re a community hub. Drop by for trivia nights, game‑themed parties, or just to chat over a glass of our signature “Wolf‑tail.” All while surrounded by the warm glow of neon lights and the comforting hum of classic soundtracks.
